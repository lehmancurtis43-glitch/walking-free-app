WALKING FREE — Your Personal Sobriety Companion

Walking Free is a warm, private, and always-with-you app designed for people in addiction recovery. Whether you're on day one or year ten, this is your calm home for every step of the journey.

TRACK YOUR SOBRIETY
Set your sobriety date and watch your progress grow in real time. Celebrate meaningful milestones — 24 hours, 30 days, 90 days, 1 year, 5 years, and beyond — with heartfelt encouragement that reminds you how far you've come.

DAILY REFLECTIONS
Start each day with strength. Read daily affirmations, inspirational quotes, and spiritual reflections that center your mind and ground your recovery.

PRIVATE JOURNAL & GRATITUDE
Write daily journal entries in a completely private space. Log gratitude moments, track your feelings, and look back on your growth over time. Your words are yours alone.

12 STEP WORK
Work the Steps with guided worksheets and reflection prompts. Track your progress through each of the 12 Steps, and keep your gratitude practice close at hand.

FIND MEETINGS
Quick links to official AA and NA meeting finders so you can connect with your local recovery community. You never have to walk alone.

PRAYER & MEDITATION
Access prayers and meditations for moments when you need peace, clarity, or spiritual connection.

YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
Walking Free works entirely offline — no sign-in required. All your data stays on your device. If you choose to sign in, your journal, milestones, and progress sync securely across all your devices so you never lose a moment of your journey.

BUILT FOR YOU
Walking Free is ad-free and built with care for the recovery community. It is not a medical device and does not replace professional treatment or therapy. If you are in crisis, please contact a healthcare professional or call emergency services.

You are stronger than you know. Keep walking free.
